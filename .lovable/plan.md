

# GOD IS ABLE BUILDING CONTRACTOR SERVICES LTD — Corporate Website

## Overview
A modern, professional 8-page corporate website for a Nigerian construction company, featuring a dark/light theme toggle, WhatsApp integration, and a contact form that submits via FormSubmit. The design uses a **deep navy blue, gold, and white** color palette with a construction industry feel.

---

## Design & Theme
- **Color Palette**: Deep navy blue (#1a2332), gold (#c8a45a), white, with dark mode support
- **Typography**: Clean, professional fonts with elegant heading hierarchy
- **Style**: Minimal animations, showroom-style layouts, mobile-first responsive design
- **Dark/Light Theme Toggle**: Accessible from the navigation bar
- **Company Logo**: Embedded throughout (navbar, footer, favicon)
